LP Commitment Memo

Fund: Theory Forge Ventures Fund I

GP/Firm: Theory Forge Ventures Management Company, LLC (website)
Fund Size: $10M (Final Close)
Vintage: 2025
Strategy: Pre-seed and Seed stage AI-focused venture capital
Date: November 16, 2025

1. Executive Summary

We committed to Theory Forge Ventures Fund I, a $10M AI-focused pre-seed and seed stage fund managed by Ben Parr (LinkedIn), and Matt Schlicht (LinkedIn). This decision was driven by three core factors: (1) the GPs’ authentic operator credibility as co-founders of Octane AI (website), a profitable B2B AI platform founded in 2016—years before the ChatGPT hype cycle 1; (2) exceptional early fund performance with $3.7M deployed into 19 companies generating 69%+ net IRR and 8 mark-ups, including standout investments like Wordware and Payman 2; and (3) differentiated deal access through their media platform (60K newsletter subscribers, 250K+ social followers) and technical evaluation capabilities that other generalist VCs lack 3.

The fund is anchored by sophisticated institutional investors including Bullpen Capital (website) and Uncork Capital (website), with participation from top-tier VCs including First Round, Spark Capital, Felicis, Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), Kleiner Perkins, and Khosla Ventures, who value Theory Forge’s AI expertise. The GPs have demonstrated strong pattern recognition and discipline, winning allocation in highly competitive rounds (pre-YC at Wordware, oversubscribed at Gumloop) while maintaining their “#1 Rule” of not investing in products they could build in a weekend 4.

As an emerging manager fund, Theory Forge carries inherent risks including key person dependency, rapid deployment pace (37% of capital already invested), and 100% sector concentration in AI. These risks are mitigated by the GPs’ 8+ years of AI operating experience, quality of co-investors validating deal selection, and early portfolio performance 5.

9. Risks & Mitigations

Key risks include: 1. Emerging manager: first-time fund, untested through full cycle 2. Key person: two principals, no larger team 3. Rapid deployment: execution and diligence risk 4. Market timing: AI hype cycle, inflated entry valuations 5. Fund size: limited follow-on capital, risk of dilution 6. 100% AI sector exposure

Mitigations include GPs’ operator experience, anchor LPs, institutional infrastructure, and specialty focus. 3637

10. Recommendation

Decision: COMMIT

Our commitment is justified by authentic AI founder track record, stand-out early results, proprietary deal access, and unique technical evaluation capabilities, validated by sophisticated institutional LPs. Risks are acknowledged and appropriately sized for an emerging manager exposure.
