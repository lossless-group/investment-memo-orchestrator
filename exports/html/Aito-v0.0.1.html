Fund: Aito Capital Fund I

GP/Firm: Aito Capital (website)

Date: November 16, 2025

1. Executive Summary

We committed capital to Aito Capital Fund I, a US-based early-stage venture fund led by Juan Abundes (LinkedIn) targeting the application layer of AI and B2B SaaS companies12. The fund writes $100K-$250K checks into 35-40 portfolio companies at pre-seed and seed stages, targeting 1-5% ownership positions with disciplined entry pricing (average $15M post-money, hard cap at $30M post-money)3.

Our investment decision was driven by three core factors: (1) Juan’s demonstrated track record deploying $300M+ across 100+ companies with notable early positions in Deel (website) ($15B valuation, 350x multiple), Ramp (website) ($7.8B valuation, 191x multiple from seed), and Unit (website) ($1.2B valuation, 96x multiple)45, (2) exceptional portfolio quality signal with 90% of prior investments raising follow-on capital and 8x MOIC across the portfolio67, and (3) differentiated sourcing through a network of 500+ operators and relationships with leading institutional investors who view Aito as a downstream signal for their follow-on capital8.

The fund’s collaborative co-investment strategy positions it as a complementary partner to larger funds rather than competing for lead positions, enabling access to competitive deals through relationship capital9. Early performance indicators from the current portfolio show multiple 2023-2024 vintage investments already achieving 1-3x markups10. The fund provides meaningful operational support focused on customer acquisition and distribution—the primary value-add that Juan leverages to win allocations in competitive rounds11.
